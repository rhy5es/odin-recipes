Description

Spaghetti spaghetti spaghetti


Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Pasta sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Italian seasoning (optional)

Steps

  1. Start by boiling the pasta until al dente or to your liking.

  2. While the pasta is cooking, start browning the ground beef in a sauce pan. Don't forget to drain the fat once it's mostly cooked through.

  3. Put your pasta sauce in the sauce pan and stir in the ground beef until the meat is well incorporated. Add Italian seasoning to your liking.

  4. Once your pasta is cooked, drain the water and put back in the pot.

  5. Feel free to mix the pasta and the sauce if you want.
