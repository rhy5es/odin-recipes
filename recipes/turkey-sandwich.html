Turkey Sandwich

Home

Description

It's a sandwich with turkey on it! Not reccomended for vegans

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Sliced deli turkey
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Butter or Mayo

Steps

  1. Get two slices of bread.
  2. Put your butter or mayo (or both if you want) on one slice of bread.
  3. On top of your butter and/or mayo, place the lettuce, tomato, and turkey.
  4. Put the remaining slice of bread on top of the stack you've just made.
