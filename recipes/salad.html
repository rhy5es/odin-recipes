Description

Salad! Yum veggies

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Carrot
  • Shredded Cheese (optional)
  • Salad Dressing (I prefer ranch, but it's up to you)

Directions

  1. Make sure all of your vegetables are clean by rinsing them under cold water and lightly scrubbing them if there's any dirt.
  2. Slice your lettuce into strips.
  3. Cut all your vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Put everything in a bowl, including your chosen salad dressing, and mix well.
